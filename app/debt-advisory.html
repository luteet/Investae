What is debt advisory?

The term ‘Debt Advisory’ is used by a variety of professionals, however not all define the service the same way. Debt Advisory is a service provided by specialists of the debt capital market, where the professional will provide guidance to a company (borrower) on how to best structure the debt financing its activity :

  • Types of debt : senior debt, loan notes,  convertibles, mezzanine...
  • Types of guarantees
  • Terms offered to investors
  • Covenants
  • Independant appraisal (credit profile)
  • Capital structure
  • Market pre-sounding
